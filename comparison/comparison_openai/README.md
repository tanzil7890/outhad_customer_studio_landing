## Core Convertive Thesis

Convertive should not position itself as a generic CDP, generic ESP, or generic personalization suite.

Its strongest wedge is:

1. Convert anonymous visitors during the live session.
2. Turn anonymous behavior into a usable profile before the visitor leaves.
3. Trigger the next best action while intent is still fresh.
4. Coordinate follow-up across channels only after the live-session opportunity has been used well.

## Market Map

| Category | Vendors | What they are strongest at | Where Convertive can win |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Identity + anonymous visitor recovery | Wunderkind, Session AI | Identity resolution, anonymous visitor recognition, behavioral AI, owned-channel recovery | Convertive can own the story around in-session intervention plus modular platform breadth across data, audiences, decisioning, orchestration, and reporting |
| Enterprise personalization suites | Dynamic Yield, Insider, Bloomreach, Nosto | Broad personalization, search, recommendations, journey orchestration, experimentation | Convertive can win on focus, speed to value, and a tighter thesis around anonymous-to-known conversion in the live session |
| Onsite conversion and Shopify personalization | Barilliance, Rebuy, Yieldify | Recommendations, popups, cart upsells, onsite merchandising, lead capture | Convertive can win by sounding more strategic and less widget-led, with stronger identity progression and next-best-action positioning |
| Data infrastructure and activation | Hightouch, Segment, RudderStack, mParticle, Adobe RT-CDP, Salesforce Data 360 | Data collection, profile unification, warehouse activation, enterprise governance | Convertive can win by being the action layer, not just the data layer |
| CRM and cross-channel engagement | Klaviyo, Braze, Attentive | Email, SMS, push, journeys, lifecycle messaging | Convertive can win by focusing on what happens before the visitor becomes reachable in owned channels |

## Best Narrative To Repeat

Use this structure across every comparison:

1. The competitor is good at its core category.
2. The problem with that category is that it often starts too late, after the visitor is already gone or already identified.
3. Convertive starts earlier, inside the live session, while the intent moment is still recoverable.
4. That makes Convertive the better choice when the buyer cares most about anonymous traffic, high-intent hesitation, and real-time conversion lift.
