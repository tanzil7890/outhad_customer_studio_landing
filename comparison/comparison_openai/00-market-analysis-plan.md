# Market Analysis Plan: Why Choose Convertive

Last updated: 2026-03-16

## Objective

Create a comparison content library that helps ecommerce operators, growth teams, and digital leaders understand why Convertive is the stronger choice when the core problem is:

- too much anonymous traffic
- too little in-session intervention
- too much reliance on next-day lifecycle campaigns
- too many disconnected tools between data, onsite action, and follow-up orchestration

## What Convertive Is Positioning Around

Based on the internal Convertive PDFs, Convertive is not just selling personalization. It is selling a specific operating model:

1. Capture live behavioral signals from the current session.
2. Build an anonymous profile immediately.
3. Infer intent and priority in real time.
4. Choose the next best action during the visit.
5. Move the visitor from anonymous to known at the right moment.
6. Keep orchestration and reporting tied to that live-session context.

That is different from platforms that start with:

- email and SMS after identity is already known
- a CDP without an in-session action layer
- on-site widgets without deeper identity progression and orchestration
- broad personalization suites whose main story is not anonymous visitor conversion

## Recommended Editorial Position

Use this editorial line in every piece:

> Most platforms either unify data, send messages, or personalize pages. Convertive is built to convert anonymous visitors while the session is still alive.

## Comparison Dimensions

| Dimension | Why it matters | Convertive angle |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Anonymous visitor handling | Most traffic is still anonymous on first visit | Convertive starts before the email is captured |
| In-session decisioning | Intent decays quickly | Convertive acts while the shopper is still browsing |
| Anonymous-to-known progression | Identity capture is a conversion event, not just a data event | Convertive frames identity capture as part of conversion strategy |
| Cross-channel orchestration | Email and SMS should follow the live session, not replace it | Convertive connects on-site intervention with downstream journeys |
| Focus | Broad suites can be powerful but diffuse | Convertive is easier to understand when the use case is conversion of anonymous traffic |
| Reporting | Teams need proof beyond vanity metrics | Convertive emphasizes identity progression, decision effectiveness, and journey lift |

## Messaging Guardrails

- Do not claim Convertive is a full replacement for every category.
- Do not attack competitors on funding, size, or brand.
- Do not promise features beyond the Convertive product story in the internal PDFs.
- Do compare based on buying sequence: before identity, during session, and after session.
- Do emphasize time-to-value, focus, and fit for ecommerce conversion teams.
