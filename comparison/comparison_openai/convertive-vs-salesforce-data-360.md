# Convertive vs Salesforce Data 360

## Bottom line

Salesforce Data 360, which many buyers still refer to as Salesforce CDP, is a large-scale activation and customer data engine native to Salesforce. Convertive is the better choice when a brand wants to solve anonymous-session conversion without taking on a broad enterprise customer data program.

## Where Salesforce Data 360 is strong

Salesforce positions Data 360 around unified customer data, real-time segmentation, activation across Customer 360, zero-copy access to enterprise data, and personalized journeys. It is well suited to organizations already deep in Salesforce.

## Why choose Convertive instead

Convertive is not trying to be the system of record for all customer data. It is trying to be the system that acts on live ecommerce intent before that intent expires. For many commerce teams, that is the more urgent purchase and the more direct path to measurable lift.

| Buying question | Convertive | Salesforce Data 360 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Main job | Convert anonymous visitors while they browse | Unify enterprise data and activate it across Salesforce |
| Best fit buyer | Ecommerce growth and digital team | Enterprise Salesforce organization |
| Strength | In-session action and identity progression | Unified data, segmentation, zero-copy activation, Customer 360 fit |
| Why pick Convertive? | Better if the priority is live-session revenue capture | Better if the priority is Salesforce-native data activation |
| Current naming note | Convertive positioning unchanged | As of 2026, Data Cloud has been rebranded to Data 360 |

## Recommended blog angle

Use a "do not buy a customer data moonshot to fix a conversion leak" theme. For many ecommerce operators, Convertive is the cleaner answer to the immediate commercial problem.
