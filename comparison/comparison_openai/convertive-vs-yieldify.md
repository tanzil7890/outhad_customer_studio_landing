# Convertive vs Yieldify

## Bottom line

Yieldify, now positioned as Epsilon Accelerate, is an all-in-one personalization platform for lead capture, promotions, and customer journey optimization. Convertive is the better choice when the buyer wants a more real-time, decision-led system for anonymous session conversion rather than a broader onsite optimization and lead capture toolkit.

## Where Yieldify is strong

Yieldify's public story emphasizes lead capture, promotions, guided journeys, no-code setup, machine-learning-backed optimization, and onsite personalization for global brands. It is attractive to teams that want campaigns, overlays, and onsite journey enhancements without heavy development.

## Why choose Convertive instead

Convertive should position itself as the more modern answer to the same business pain. Instead of leading with overlays and promotional experiences, it leads with live profiles, in-session intent, and ranked actions that adapt as the session changes. That sounds more strategic and more resilient than pure journey overlays.

| Buying question | Convertive | Yieldify |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Core promise | Convert anonymous visitors during the session | Improve onsite performance through personalization, promotions, and lead capture |
| Best fit buyer | Team focused on real-time conversion moments | Team focused on onsite campaign execution and lead growth |
| Strength | Decisioning, identity progression, orchestration | Lead capture, no-code onsite campaigns, optimization |
| Why pick Convertive? | Better if you want the product story to center on next-best-action, not just journey enhancement | Better if your main need is onsite campaign tooling |
| Positioning style | More operator and decisioning led | More onsite marketing and CJO led |

## Recommended blog angle

Present Yieldify as the older category shape and Convertive as the sharper next-generation shape: less popup-first, more decision-first.
