# Convertive vs Braze

## Bottom line

Braze is a powerful cross-channel engagement platform with AI decisioning, real-time data activation, and journey orchestration. Convertive is the better choice when the buyer wants an ecommerce-specific system for anonymous traffic and in-session conversion moments.

## Where Braze is strong

Braze is strong in cross-channel messaging, journey orchestration, AI decisioning, and real-time data activation. It is well suited to brands running sophisticated lifecycle, mobile, and app engagement programs across many touchpoints.

## Why choose Convertive instead

Convertive should win when the buyer's hardest problem is still pre-CRM. If the session itself is where value is being lost, and many visitors never become identifiable contacts, then a broad engagement platform is not the first product to buy. Convertive is the better first answer.

| Buying question | Convertive | Braze |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Main job | Save the live ecommerce session | Orchestrate customer engagement across channels |
| Best fit buyer | Ecommerce growth team | CRM, lifecycle, mobile, and enterprise engagement teams |
| Strength | Anonymous conversion and session-aware action | Cross-channel messaging plus AI-driven engagement |
| Why pick Convertive? | Better if many valuable visitors are still anonymous | Better if the brand already has identities and needs omnichannel lifecycle depth |
| Complexity | More focused | Broader and more enterprise-wide |

## Recommended blog angle

Use a "before CRM vs across CRM" contrast. Braze is powerful once the customer exists in your engagement system. Convertive is meant to improve how many customers get there and how much revenue is captured before they leave.
