# Convertive vs Attentive

## Bottom line

Attentive is strongest in AI-powered SMS, email, list growth, and audience activation. Convertive is the better choice when a brand wants to improve what happens before list growth and messaging: the anonymous visitor's live-session experience.

## Where Attentive is strong

Attentive's public positioning centers on SMS, email, list growth, visitor identification, audience management, and AI-powered messaging. It is very strong when the goal is to grow subscribers and improve channel performance in owned messaging.

## Why choose Convertive instead

Convertive goes earlier in the funnel. Instead of starting with sign-up units and then messaging, it starts with behavior, intent, hesitation, and action selection while the user is still browsing. That makes it more aligned with conversion optimization than list-growth-first platforms.

| Buying question | Convertive | Attentive |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Main job | Convert and identify visitors during the session | Grow lists and activate SMS and email effectively |
| Best fit buyer | Site conversion and growth team | Retention and owned-channel team |
| Strength | In-session decisioning and anonymous progression | SMS, email, audience management, sign-up experiences |
| Why pick Convertive? | Better if the team wants to improve the live session before handoff to channels | Better if subscriber growth and channel execution are the top priorities |
| Can they work together? | Yes | Yes |

## Recommended blog angle

Use a "capture the moment before capture the subscriber" message. Attentive is strong after the decision to subscribe. Convertive aims to influence the behavior that leads to that decision.
